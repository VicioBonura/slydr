Multiple gradients

You can declare multiple gradients in the same statement by separating them with commas; they will be arranged in overlapping layers. You can use the alpha channel and background properties to make each layer visible and position it.
background-image:
 repeating-conic-gradient(from 0deg at 80px 0,
 #ffff7700 0deg, #ffffdd33 15deg, #ffff7700 20deg),
 radial-gradient(#ff3333, #dd0000 24px, #dd000000 25px),
 radial-gradient(ellipse at center,
  #000000 0px 10px, #00000033 25px, #00000000 25px),
 radial-gradient(circle at 80px 0px,
  #ffff9e 20px, #ffff9e00 100px),
 linear-gradient(to bottom, #a5ffff, #ffffff 33%, #d0fdff 33%, #4facfe 66%, #ffd6b5 66%, #ffe7d3 100%);
background-size:100%, 50px 50px, 60px 15px, 100%, 100%;
background-repeat: no-repeat;
background-position:center, 250px 250px, 253px 290px, center, center;
