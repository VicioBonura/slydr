Anatomy of a statement

text-shadow: 2px 3px 15px rgba(0,0,0,0.5);
box-shadow: inset 2px 3px 15px 5px rgba(0,0,0,0.5);

Values

  • position: (optional) if specified, the shadow is applied to the inside of the element, outside otherwise
  • offset-x: (required, default: 0) the horizontal offset. Positive values move the shadow to the right
  • offset-y: (required, default: 0) the vertical offset. Positive values move the shadow down
  • blur-radius: (optional, default: 0) the blur value. Negative values are not allowed
  • spread-radius: (optional, default: 0) the spread value. Negative values shrink the shadow
  • color: (optional, default: parent color property) the color of the shadow
NOTE:
The text-shadow property does not support inner and spread values.
Both properties accept multiple values, separated by commas.
