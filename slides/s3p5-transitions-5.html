Multiple transitions

transition-property: top, left, width, height, background;
transition-duration: 5s, 4s, 2s, 2s, 2s;
transition-timing-function: ease;
transition-delay: 0s, 1s, 2s, 3s, 3s;
/* using shorthand */
transition:
 top 5s ease,
 left 4s ease 1s,
 width 2s ease 2s,
 height 2s ease 3s,
 background 2s ease 3s;

Hover over the dashed area to start the transitions
